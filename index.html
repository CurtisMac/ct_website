Doula \ ˈdü-lə \

“A trained professional who provides continuous physical, emotional and informational support to a mother before, during and shortly after childbirth to help her achieve the healthiest, most satisfying experience possible.” - www.dona.org

What does a doula do?

A doula provides compassionate, comforting care, before, during and after you give birth.

  • Provide compassionate, comforting care
  • Help you envision your ideal birth
  • Keep you comfortable until you check into the hospital or until your midwife joins you for your planned home birth
  • Help you prepare your home for your home birth and help with the clean up afterwards
  • Get you comfortable in the hospital should you chose to birth in the hospital setting
  • Support your partner through the birth
  • Remind you to breath, to relax to trust your body
  • Provide soothing massages, hip squeezes and back rubs to help you relax and bring you comfort
  • Assist and encourage successful breastfeeding
  • Help each mother feel confident and empowered by her unique birth journey

About me

Each woman’s birth experience is uniquely her own, it is intimate and personal. As a Doula it is a privilege to share in the birth process with a woman and her partner. I have had the joy of sharing the birth experience with several women and look forward to supporting many more strong, informed women who have thought through their wants and desires as they bring their precious babies to life. Recently I completed the Dona birth doula training at Mothering Touch in Victoria and continue to pursue my Doula certification through Dona International.
